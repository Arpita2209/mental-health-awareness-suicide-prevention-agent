Suicide Prevention

Recognise warning signs and know how to respond compassionately.

The 4-7-8 technique activates your body's calm response. Follow the circle.

Ready
Press Start to begin
Round 0 / 4
  • 🌿
    Ground yourself (5-4-3-2-1): Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste. Interrupts anxiety spirals instantly.
  • 💬
    Talk to someone you trust: Sharing feelings with a friend, family member, or counselor reduces emotional burden significantly.
  • 🚶
    Move your body: A 20-minute walk triggers endorphins. Regular movement is one of the most effective natural antidepressants.
  • 📵
    Limit social media: Heavy social media use is linked to higher depression and anxiety. Schedule screen-free time daily.
  • 😴
    Prioritise sleep: Aim for 7–9 hours. Sleep and mental health are deeply intertwined — poor sleep amplifies all symptoms.
  • 📓
    Gratitude journaling: Writing three things you're grateful for each day rewires neural pathways toward positivity over time.
